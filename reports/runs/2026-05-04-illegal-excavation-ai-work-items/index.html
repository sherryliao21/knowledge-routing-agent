Business Summary

This meeting outlined the key features for our new system, including how Artificial Intelligence will help identify potential issues from imagery, significant improvements to our mapping tools, and an enhanced process for tracking follow-up actions. A critical component is the migration of our existing file management system to ensure we meet security compliance standards. The project is currently in the bidding phase, with an expected mid-May kick-off and an immediate need for detailed requirements discussions upon bid award.

Key Business Outcomes
Significantly improves efficiency by automating the initial identification of potential issues using AI.
Enhances decision-making and user experience with advanced mapping features, including historical data review and adjustable imagery.
Ensures data integrity and operational efficiency by preventing duplicate image uploads and providing structured input for survey data.
Addresses critical information security and compliance requirements by migrating the legacy file management system.
Streamlines workflow and accountability with new features for tracking, dispatching, and reporting on follow-up actions.
