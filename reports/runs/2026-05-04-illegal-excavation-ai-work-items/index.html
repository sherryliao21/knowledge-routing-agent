Business Summary

This meeting outlined the core features for our new system, focusing on how artificial intelligence will analyze images and how our teams will manage and follow up on the findings. A critical goal of this project is to upgrade our information security and improve operational efficiency. We anticipate a project kick-off in mid-May, with immediate requirements discussions following the successful bid.

Key Business Outcomes
Significantly improve our information security posture by upgrading the legacy file management system.
Automate and streamline the process of identifying potential issues through AI-driven image analysis, making operations more efficient.
Provide users with a powerful, intuitive map viewer to easily review historical images and track suspected locations.
Enhance project oversight and accountability with integrated tools for managing and tracking all follow-up actions and survey reports.
Offer flexible options for inputting image data for AI analysis, supporting both large-scale regional data and individual user uploads.
