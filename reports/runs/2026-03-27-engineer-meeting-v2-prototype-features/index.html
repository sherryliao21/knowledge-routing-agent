Stakeholder Executive Briefing

Business-focused summary: impact, alignment decisions, and top open questions.

Business Summary

The initial version (V1) of our new system will focus on two core functionalities: managing photo uploads and generating 3D models. Users will log in seamlessly using our existing external portal, eliminating the need for separate registration within this system. We have prioritized a clear and simple user experience for the first release, deferring more advanced features to future development.

Key Business Outcomes (4)
Users will experience streamlined access, leveraging their existing external login without a new registration process.
The core value proposition of photo management and 3D model generation will be delivered in the first release, providing immediate utility.
The V1 user experience will be kept simple with default settings for 3D modeling, reducing initial complexity for users.
Future enhancements such as project sharing, advanced upload options, and customizable modeling parameters have been clearly planned for subsequent versions, ensuring a focused V1 launch.

Open Questions (1)
No major unresolved questions are currently blocking the V1 project, as the core functionalities and scope have been clearly defined and confirmed.
