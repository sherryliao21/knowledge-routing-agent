Business Summary

This meeting clarified key features for the new monitoring system, focusing on how artificial intelligence (AI) will interpret images, improvements to the interactive map, and the necessary migration of an older system to meet security standards. These updates aim to enhance our ability to efficiently detect, monitor, and follow up on potential issues using visual data and AI. We are targeting a project kick-off in mid-May, pending successful bidding.

Key Business Outcomes
AI image analysis will run automatically in the background, promptly notifying users when potential issues are detected, which should accelerate initial identification.
Users will have improved tools to review AI-identified points and place them under monitoring, streamlining the process of confirming and acting on critical findings.
The system will support flexible image input, accommodating both large nationwide datasets and individual user uploads for AI analysis.
Migrating our legacy file management system is essential to ensure compliance with current information security requirements.
The enhanced map viewer will provide better visualization of historical imagery and suspected points, aiding in comprehensive analysis and decision-making.
New follow-up features will enable more efficient tracking and reporting on identified issues, ensuring they are addressed systematically.
