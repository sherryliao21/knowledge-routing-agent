Stakeholder Executive Briefing

Business-focused summary: impact, alignment decisions, and top open questions.

Business Summary

We're moving forward with developing a new platform aimed at enhancing livestock disease prevention and management. Key decisions include integrating data from the Livestock Disease Control Center (LDCC) and planning for the system's long-term maintenance by our business unit after an initial two-year period of on-site data entry. This project will significantly improve our ability to respond to outbreaks and manage livestock data more effectively.

Key Business Outcomes (5)
Enhanced ability to quickly identify and respond to livestock disease outbreaks within specific geographical areas.
Streamlined data sharing and analysis across different livestock-related datasets and external departments, improving overall operational efficiency.
Increased transparency and accountability through system logs and a clear process for reporting data errors.
Long-term cost savings as the business unit will take over system maintenance after an initial operational period, reducing reliance on external teams.
Empowers local township offices with essential read-only access to relevant livestock information.
